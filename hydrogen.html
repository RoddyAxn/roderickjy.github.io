Waikato Hydrogen Strategy

Roderick John Young supports investigating hydrogen as part of Waikato’s long-term energy future.

  • Hydrogen for heavy freight
  • Backup energy resilience
  • Local fuel production
  • Hamilton–Huntly–Tauranga energy corridor
  • Partnership with councils, iwi, government, and tertiary institutions

Hydrogen can turn surplus Waikato electricity into stored local fuel.
